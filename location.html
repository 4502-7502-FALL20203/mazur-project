7 Best Coffee Shops in Columbia, Missouri

Columbia, Missouri is overflowing with college students. Whether it’s a Wednesday night or an early Sunday morning, you’re bound to find them out on the town partying or studying (with rarely much in between). To accommodate all those cramming for their courses, Columbia has also become a hub for coffee shops. Here are seven of the best, rated for their coffee and atmosphere.

7. Aroma Coffee House

1009 N Providence Rd, Columbia, MO 65203

Aroma Coffee Shop Facebook Page

(573) 228-9480

Although the coffee at Aroma isn’t top tier, the delicious Venezuelan food, large board game selection, and discounted vinyl sales make this a great visit for an afternoon snack or hangout sesh.

6. Fretboard Coffee

1013 E Walnut St, Columbia, MO 65201

Fretboard Coffee

(573) 227-2233

Fretboard Coffee only offers a few special flavors every few weeks, so it can sometimes be hit or miss depending on what’s available. But when it hits, boy, it hits!

5. The Grind South

4306 John Garry Drive Suite 1 Columbia, MO 65203

The Grind South Website

(636) 465-0538

A delicious, extensive menu, complete with fun seasonal flavors. The Grind South also gets huge bonus points for being one of the only places open until midnight in town — perfect for the late-night grind (no pun intended) with your study buddies.

4. Uprise Bakery

10 Hitt St, Columbia, MO 65201

Uprise Bakery Website

(573) 256-2265

A coffee shop, a bar, a café and a movie theater all rolled into one. Do I need to say anything else? Plus, they have the most scrumptious iced maple latte I’ve ever had.

3. Acola Coffee Co.

300 N 10th St Suite 100, Columbia, MO 65201

Acola Coffee Website

(573) 447-8050

Formerly known as Three Story, Acola Coffee has the fanciest latte art you will ever see, along with some of the creamiest blends. A great, airy atmosphere complete with plants that you can buy!

2. Toasty Goat Coffee Co.

515 S Scott Blvd Ste 101, Columbia, MO 65203

Toasty Goat Coffee Website

(573) 607-9015

Toasty Goat has the most carefully handcrafted beautiful atmosphere. You’ll want to stay here for hours to listen to music, enjoy the natural light, read, and enjoy sweet coffee. If you stick around long enough some nights, there’s even live music.

1. Coffee Zone

11 N 9th St, Columbia, MO 65201

Coffee Zone Website

(573) 449-8215

Coffee Zone tops this list because of its iced Vietnamese cold brew, which beats any other coffee easily with its rich, mocha-like taste. The Greek food also makes for a delicious, savory midday snack.
