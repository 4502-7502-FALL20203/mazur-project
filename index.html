my recent work

  • Price of Plenty fertilizer project

    This article highlights the effects of fertilizer on the Gulf of Mexico through a profile on the researcher who publicized the problem of the "Dead Zone." It was written as part of a collaboration with the University of Missouri, the University of Florida, the Mississippi River Basin Ag & Water Desk, NPR and the Pulitzer Center.

  • Articles for the International League Against Epilepsy

    As an intern for the ILAE news journal, Epigraph, I write about various aspects of epilepsy for an audience of professionals.

  • Articles for the Columbia Missourian

    I have written for the Columbia Missourian since 2021. My stories have covered a variety of beats, including community, environmental and state government.
